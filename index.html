Meet Theo

A seasoned professional with a profound impact on human resources and economic development across the African continent.

Fondly known as Theo, Mr. Chukwudi Theophilus Nwamadi stands at the helm of Interim Consulting Pty Ltd, a leading human resources services provider. His strategic vision has cultivated enduring partnerships with prominent blue-chip organizations throughout South Africa and Nigeria, solidifying Interim Consulting's reputation for excellence.

Beyond his corporate leadership, Theo is a dedicated advocate for African progress. His influential roles in diplomatic relations, community initiatives, and significant contributions to attracting foreign investment underscore his commitment to fostering growth and opportunity across the region.

Journey & Impact

Key milestones and contributions shaping a distinguished career dedicated to progress in Africa.

Early Career

Academic & Literary Roots

Graduated from Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria; Lectured at Borno College of Science and Technology; Served as Editor-in-Chief of the Student Union magazine; Authored over 22 articles in Nigerian newspapers.

1994

Relocation to South Africa

Moved to South Africa, inspired by the country's potential and opportunities for growth and contribution.

1995

National Essay Competition Win

Won the prestigious National Essay Competition with his piece "SA – Emerging Role in Africa," demonstrating early foresight and analytical skill.

Leadership Roles

Industry & Diplomatic Service

Served on the Boards of South Africa's SETA Services and Africa Personnel Services Organization (APSO); Contributed to diplomatic and community relations as a Council Member at the Nigeria High Commission.

Community Advocacy

Patriots Club President

Led the Patriots Club of Nigeria International, championing development initiatives and socio-cultural progress across the continent.

Literary Contribution

Co-Authored "God Did Not Come to the Party"

Co-authored a powerful and inspiring book, a rallying call for African youth to drive their own sustainable future.

Recognition

Honored with the prestigious chieftaincy title, “Ome k’ahuru anya I,” by Mgbirichi and Abakuru Autonomous Communities in Imo State, Nigeria, recognizing his contributions to community development.
